SKIING

Freestyle Skiing
Freestyle skiing is a thrilling sport where athletes perform tricks and aerial maneuvers on snow. It includes events like moguls, aerials, slopestyle, and halfpipe, with skiers judged on creativity, skill, and execution. The sport combines athleticism and artistry and has been in the Winter Olympics since 1992.

<

Laax, Mammoth, Avoriaz, and Whistler are top snowparks for freestyle skiing and snowboarding. Laax is famous for its huge halfpipes and creative features. Mammoth offers long seasons and diverse terrain parks. Avoriaz is known for its accessible, well-groomed parks in the French Alps. Whistler boasts world-class parks with varied jumps, rails, and stunning mountain scenery.
